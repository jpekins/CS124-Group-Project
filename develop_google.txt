Covers two trays with baking paper and leave to one side. In a shallow bowl, mix brown sugar and pecans. It covers a dyke with foil and crush the dumplings, one at a time, and fill them with 1 1/2 tablespoons of caramel. You can also use a plate to crush the pellets. With the help of the foil, carefully folding the ends and closed the border to arm empanadas. Keep the balls of dough covered so they do not dry out. Cool slightly before serving. Enjoy them warm or at room temperature. Let cool completely before storing in an airtight container. They have a duration of 3-5 days.
