Covers two trays with baking paper and leave to one side. In a shallow bowl, mix brown sugar and pecans. It covers a dyke with foil and crush the dumplings, one at a time, and fill them with 1 1/2 tablespoons of caramel. You can also use a plate to crush the pellets. With the help of the foil, carefully folding the ends and closed the border to arm empanadas. Keep the balls of dough covered so they do not dry out. Spend pies for mixtures of sugar and pecans and give slight pat the sugar from sticking to each side. Spend pies to trays. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until slightly browned. Maybe you have to move the tray from the bottom up and bake a few more minutes after the tray up this list. Is likely to fall over the sides while caramel pies are baked. Cool slightly before serving. Enjoy them warm or at room temperature. Let cool completely before storing in an airtight container. They have a duration of 3-5 days.
